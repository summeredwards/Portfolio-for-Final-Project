ABOUT


Hello, and welcome to my personal portfolio. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Colorado Boulder studying for my Bachelor of Science degree in Technology, Arts, and Media. My entire life I have been extremely passionate about science, math, and art. While I love learning facts, I have always enjoyed stretching my creativity. Both of these qualities work well for my major, and have helped me develop great analytical and creative problem solving skills.

On a more personal level, I was born and raised in Colorado and graduated from Regis Jesuit High School. I enjoy almost any kind of art imaginable. I love to paint, draw, using multimedia, photography, and I am currently teaching myself how to sew. I have gone on multiple art trips to Europe with my school and have fallen in love with travel. I am hoping to further my education abroad and immerse myself in different cultures.
